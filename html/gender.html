History

Musangi $ Vosty Foundation was founded in 2022 courtesy of Erick Ochieng, Augo Isaiah, Robinson Obatha, Winfred Mwende, Benard Odiwour, Kyalo and Vosty.

It was founded as a means to interact more with the society and to connect deeply with every needy member of the society.

We host various functions where we interacting with the community.

We have done the mentioned activities through visiting schools in our society, hosting meetings where we empower members of our society, participating in sport activities as well as hosting the activities.

We have visit various homes, schools and churches to ensure we interact deeply with the whole community.

We have done environmental activities that help maintain a clean and responsible society such as tree planting and environmental cleaning.
